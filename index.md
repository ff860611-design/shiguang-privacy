# 食光 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

食光 is developed by Feng Fu.

## Data We Collect
- User preferences (dietary restrictions, taste preferences) stored locally on your device
- Ingredient data you enter, stored locally on your device
- We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers

## Camera & Photos
Camera and photo library access is used only to recognize ingredients from photos.
Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for processing and are not stored by us.

## Third Party Services
We use Google Gemini AI API to generate recipe recommendations.
Please refer to Google's privacy policy for their data practices.
